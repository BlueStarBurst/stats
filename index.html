The Stats I Used

The three statistics that I used were the total reported gross income from their latest tour, current number of monthly listeners on spotify, and the total units sold for their most recent album. I chose these statistics because I think that they give a good representation of current popularity.

I found these means by adding up the data from each stat and diving by 5 (the number of bands) and I found the standard deviations by plugging in the data in my calculator and doing 1-var stats because I'm lazy.

I found these z-scores by using the stats, values, and formula from the last couple page.

Composite Scores

The composite score is the sum of the all the z-scores.

  1. Ed Sheeran: 4.542

  2. BTS: 0.011

  3. Taylor Swift: -0.497

  4. Beyoncé: -1.78

  5. Panic! At the Disco: -2.275

Conclusion

The GOAT for current, popular musicians is Ed Sheeran with an outstanding composite score of 4.542.
